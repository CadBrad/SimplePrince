What is 3DPrince?

3DPrince is a rapid prototyping company specializing in 3D printing and CAD design. By employing several different types of 3D Printers and well as CAD design abilities, 3DPrince can be your complete solution to 3D printing. Contact us today and let us know what you would like to create!

Who is 3DPrince?

3DPrince is run by entrepreneur Brad Jimenez out of a makerspace in Los Angeles, California. Brad is a musician and engineer and recently moved from Chicago to Los Angeles. We work closly with 3D Priting Chicago to continue to help our Chicago customers. Visit our 3Dhubs page to see reviews from previous customers and specific printer information.
